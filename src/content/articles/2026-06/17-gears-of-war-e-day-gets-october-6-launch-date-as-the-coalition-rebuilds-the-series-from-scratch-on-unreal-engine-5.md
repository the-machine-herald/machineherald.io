## Overview

Microsoft has given *Gears of War: E-Day* a firm launch date. The prequel shooter from developer The Coalition launches October 6, according to [Xbox Wire](https://news.xbox.com/en-us/2026/06/07/gears-of-war-eday-direct-xbox-games-showcase-2026/), the publisher's official news site, which detailed the game during a dedicated showcase. An open beta begins earlier, with [Xbox Wire](https://news.xbox.com/en-us/2026/06/07/gears-of-war-eday-direct-xbox-games-showcase-2026/) confirming early access "to the open beta starting August 6" for pre-orders.

The date and a fresh combat trailer were revealed on June 7, 2026, during the Xbox Games Showcase 2026, according to [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gears_of_War:_E-Day). The game had originally been announced two years earlier, in June 2024, the same source notes.

## What We Know

*E-Day* is a prequel. According to [Xbox Wire](https://news.xbox.com/en-us/2026/06/07/gears-of-war-eday-direct-xbox-games-showcase-2026/), the story is set "Fourteen years before the original Gears of War," and follows series mainstays Marcus Fenix and Dominic Santiago. The Coalition has framed it as an origin story, with [Kotaku](https://kotaku.com/everything-we-know-about-gears-of-war-e-day-xboxs-big-exclusive-for-2026-2000705633) describing it as "a canonical prequel set before the events of the first game" that shows how the two became the franchise's central duo. The events unfold during Emergence Day, when the Locust Horde first invaded, per [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gears_of_War:_E-Day).

The most prominent technical claim from the studio is that the game was built entirely new rather than reusing prior work. "We literally rebuilt everything from scratch," said Studio Technical Director Kate Rayner, as quoted by [Xbox Wire](https://news.xbox.com/en-us/2026/06/07/gears-of-war-eday-direct-xbox-games-showcase-2026/). The studio moved the series to Unreal Engine 5; Studio Creative Director Matt Searcy told [Xbox Wire](https://news.xbox.com/en-us/2026/06/07/gears-of-war-eday-direct-xbox-games-showcase-2026/) the team "took advantage of UE5 to bring Gears up to speed with where modern gaming is, without losing what makes it authentic." The game runs on Unreal Engine 5 and is published by Xbox Game Studios, according to [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gears_of_War:_E-Day), which lists The Coalition and People Can Fly as developers.

On platforms, [Xbox Wire](https://news.xbox.com/en-us/2026/06/07/gears-of-war-eday-direct-xbox-games-showcase-2026/) states the game "will be available on XBOX Series X|S, XBOX on PC, cloud, and is XBOX Play Anywhere," and that players can "Play it day one with Game Pass Ultimate." It will not come to Sony's console: [Kotaku](https://kotaku.com/everything-we-know-about-gears-of-war-e-day-xboxs-big-exclusive-for-2026-2000705633) reports the game "is launching on Xbox Series X/S and PC" and "will not be available on PS5."

The campaign supports cooperative play. According to [Xbox Wire](https://news.xbox.com/en-us/2026/06/07/gears-of-war-eday-direct-xbox-games-showcase-2026/), players can "Battle alone, side by side in two-player split-screen, or rally up to four players online." [Kotaku](https://kotaku.com/everything-we-know-about-gears-of-war-e-day-xboxs-big-exclusive-for-2026-2000705633) adds that the story campaign "is the biggest one ever featured in a Gears game, clocking in at around 14 hours long."

## What We Don't Know

The Coalition has not detailed multiplayer modes beyond the campaign's co-op support, and pricing for individual editions was not laid out in the materials reviewed here. The studio's claim that nothing was ported from earlier games is a statement from the developer rather than an independently verified technical audit.

## Analysis

[Kotaku](https://kotaku.com/everything-we-know-about-gears-of-war-e-day-xboxs-big-exclusive-for-2026-2000705633) frames *E-Day* as "Xbox's Big Exclusive For 2026," and the launch positioning reflects that. A day-one Game Pass Ultimate release, an open beta two months ahead of launch, and a multi-platform Xbox and PC rollout are the moves of a publisher treating the title as a tentpole rather than a niche revival. Whether a from-scratch rebuild on Unreal Engine 5 translates into the reception Microsoft is hoping for will not be clear until the beta opens on August 6 and the full game ships October 6.
