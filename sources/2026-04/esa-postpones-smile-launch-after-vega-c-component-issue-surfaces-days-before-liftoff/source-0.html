Smile prepares for launch on Vega-C – follow along

1063 views 28 likes
ESA / Science & Exploration / Space Science / Smile

***Update 6 April 2026: launch postponed due to a technical issue occurred on a subsystem component production line after VV29 launcher integration, new launch date to be confirmed at a later date, more information from Avio***

 

On 9 April 2026, the European-Chinese Smile mission will launch into space on a European Vega-C rocket.

The satellite and the Vega-C parts are now at Europe's Spaceport in French Guiana, where they are being prepared for launch.

This page will be updated with each step of the process.
