CX Enterprise Coworker FAQs.

What is AI marketing workflow automation?

AI marketing workflow automation uses artificial intelligence to handle repetitive, rules-based marketing tasks (like routing requests, creating campaigns from briefs, or triggering journeys), so teams can focus on strategy and creativity instead of manual operations.

How does Adobe CX Enterprise Coworker improve marketing efficiency and performance?

CX Enterprise Coworker streamlines end-to-end marketing work by acting as an AI teammate that supports campaigns, automates approvals and handoffs, and surfaces real-time insights. It helps teams launch faster, reduce errors, and keep projects on time and on budget while maintaining governance and auditability.

What types of marketing workflows can be automated with Adobe CX Enterprise Coworker?

It can automate campaign and project setup, intake and routing of briefs and requests, task and approval workflows, content review and feedback cycles, status reporting, and basic analytics and insights across channels.

How does CX Enterprise Coworker differ from traditional agents?

Traditional agents complete singular tasks using question-and-answer interactions. CX Enterprise Coworker is able to take multiple actions after a goal and parameters have been defined. Acting as an AI collaborator embedded within planning, production, and workflow tools, it can help teams execute customer experience orchestration tasks across the entire customer lifecycle.

How does CX Enterprise Coworker connect with Adobe Experience Platform Agent Orchestrator?

CX Enterprise Coworker is powered by Adobe’s agentic “harness”, Adobe Agent Orchestrator. This capability is integrated into our industry-leading applications and has been designed to interoperate with other agentic platforms.

How does CX Enterprise Coworker connect with Adobe’s specialized agents?

Adobe’s specialized agents are complementary to CX Enterprise Coworker. While individual agents, such as Audience Agent and Journey Agent, focus on specific tasks, CX Enterprise Coworker serves as the team captain, orchestrating customer experience workflows across agents and marketers. Purpose-built agents are typically invoked to complete a specific task and then terminate. In contrast, CX Enterprise Coworker operates with persistent agency established by marketing leaders. It executes business workflows aligned to established goals and continuously learns from past interactions to improve performance over time.

How does CX Enterprise Coworker connect with AI Assistant, which is a part of Adobe products?

AI Assistant is the interface that customers can interact with to access Adobe’s agentic capabilities.
