WPP, Publicis and Dentsu Settle Alleged Political Ad Boycotts

April 15, 2026, 4:31 PM UTC
Leah Nylen
Bloomberg News

Advertising agencies WPP Plc, Publicis Groupe SA and Dentsu Group Inc. agreed to not coordinate on ad buying, settling a US Federal Trade Commission complaint, the agency said.

The FTC and eight states alleged the companies illegally colluded under the guise of targeting misinformation. The complaint alleged that instead of promulgating neutral rules, industry groups instead targeted specific views and conservative websites.
